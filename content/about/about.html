Noah Shipping & Logistics Ltd is a leading local and international logistics solutions provider for private and public sector players. We support trade and industry through land transport, worldwide air freight, ocean freight, contract logistics and supply chain solutions. Our full suite of services include: International Freight Forwarding, Cargo Handling, CFS Operations, Clearing and Forwarding, Transportation, Warehousing and Distribution, Insurance and Courier Services.

Our business holds top interests in the automotive, technology, consumer goods, construction, pharmaceutical, humanitarian, special transports and dangerous goods industries. We provide you with regular updates of your orders, quotations, invoices, inventory and delivery schedules.
