About Us

At Noah Shipping & Logistics Ltd, we facilitate seamless trade and commerce through our diverse range of services. Our expertise spans land transport, global air freight, ocean freight, contract logistics, and sophisticated supply chain solutions.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the preferred one-stop logistics solutions partner.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer value-adding global logistical and advisory services to public and private entities. The Noah Shipping & Logistics service mantra is logistics without borders.

Noah Shipping & Logistics has three strategically located domestic hubs – The head office at Crater Automobiles Building on Mombasa Road, a major transit highway with Express Way in Nairobi; The branch office with CFS Operations inside the Inland Container Depot (ICD), a stone’s throw from the Jomo Kenyatta International Airport; and, The Cargo Handling facility (Kenya Ports Authority Back Port Shed 1) inside the Port of Mombasa and CFS operations located at Port Reize area barely 2kms from the port of Mombasa.

By operating in one of the key transit points for logistics in East and Central Africa, Noah Shipping & Logistics connects the most important economic regions in more than eight African countries – Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Ethiopia, South Sudan, Rwanda, Burundi, and Eastern DRC. Noah Shipping & Logistics is a certified Authorized Economic Operator (AEO) as accredited by the Kenya Revenue Authority – Customs.
